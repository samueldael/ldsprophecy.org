The Seven Last Plagues

Undeterminable without Revelation from God

One must understand that it is only speculation when we try to interpret the seven last plagues. Prophecy can only be understood through reason after the event has transpired. Even if a prophet could see, the Lord would most likely say, “Seal if Up.” That was the reason for holding out until the time of the end until all had bee fulfilled that the saints could see sufficiently to redeem Zion without hand.

Articles may follow in time, but rest assured they will be speculation, but based on the knowledge of prophecy and history, they might come close or at least in the proper direction.

The point here will be to emphasis the importance of this warning. It is not to avoid the Seven Last Plagues, but to live through them. After viewing the image, you should see that the Redemption of Zion starts soon. This is the establishment “again” of the Holy Order of the Son of God that the early Mormon Gentiles rejected according to prophecy. Those interested must redeem Zion without hand, which probably means without revelation, but not without knowledge—the purpose of ldsprophecy.org. We must prove ourselves before we can see the face of the Lord. When we live his Holy Order, we will again talk with God face to face, as did all the prophets until we fail again and again to follow the fullness of the gospel of Jesus Christ and stop turning it into something easy that only believing certain ways or certain authority, can make it possible. We must lean obedience to the Laws of Heaven.
